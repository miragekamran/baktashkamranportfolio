Basement Floor Plan

The above image represents basement floor plan which is assigned for buffet system dining and IT department plus server rooms and HR offices. Staff can access to the basement both through the elevator and the stairwell case, the stairwell case is designed as a fire rated stairs which can be used for emergency evacuations as well. PVC windows are located in the basement to have natural lighting during the day.

First Floor Plan

The above image represents the first or ground floor plan, which is assigned for both security and Logistics Divisions, A separate office is designed for Director of the Division and other partitions are set up for the Logistic staff that could be separated by a pony wall. Building entrance is on the top left corner of the image, the room at the entrance is assigned as an information and security check kiosk. Staff can access to the upper floors through the elevator and the stairs. Two separate restrooms for both gens are assigned at each floor.
