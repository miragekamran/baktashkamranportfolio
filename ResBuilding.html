Basement and First Floor Plans

Basement is laid out to be protected from moisture and cold during winter season, and the first floor is consisting of a master bedroom, a bathroom, a separate kitchen and a living and dining area, a circular stairs are designed to get access to the basement and the upper floors.

Second and Third Floor Plans

The above images are second and third floors. There are four bedrooms laid out in the second floor including two master bedrooms and two single bedroom plus a bathroom in the hallway the same circular stairs are connected to the first and the third floors. significant areas are designed for the balconies on the north and south side of the building for three bedrooms. Two bedrooms are designed on the third floor plus a huge open area as a patio for both rooms and the same circular stairs are connecting residents to the second floor and the top roof of the building.

Roof Plan and Cross Section

The Image on the top right side of the page shows cross section of the building to show all the framing and other partition material on the whole building, the foundation for the basement is well compacted and insulated for seasonal surface water for all the finishing please zoom in the picture to see all the materials listed on each floor. A water reservoir is located on the top roof of the building right on the circular stair case to make the elevation of the building more interesting.
